Queen

Years Active: 1970 - Present

Purpose: The purpose of this page will be to focus on the band Queen, one of the most popular bands of the age of rock. There will be much more description of this band, as well as their background, the background of each member, and their inspiration.

Actions: There will be links to some hit songs from this band, as well as links to interviews so the user can learn more about specific artists who made rock what it is today. This page will not be made necessarily to promote individual bands themselves, but to promote the user into learning more about music and it's history, and just how diverse and how many genres and artists make up music as we know it.
